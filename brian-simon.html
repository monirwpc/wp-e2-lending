Brian Simon, CMB

Senior Vice President and Director of Mortgage Banking

Brian is responsible for all day-to-day operations and strategic execution of our mortgage banking platform.

Before joining First Savings Bank, Mr. Simon was an executive at Altisource and served as President of the Lenders One, Trelix and Castleline business units. Previously, Brian has held C-level leadership positions at some of the largest mortgage companies in the industry, including:

  • Chief Operating Officer of New Penn Financial (now known as New Rez)
  • Chief Executive Officer of Caliber Home Loans
  • Chief Operating Officer of Freedom Mortgage

Brian has been a long-time member of both the Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac advisory boards, holds the prestigious Certified Mortgage Banker designation, and has been voted one of the top one hundred mortgage banking executives in the industry.
