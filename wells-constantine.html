Wells Constantine

Vice President and TPO National Sales Manager

Wells is responsible for supporting and managing the TPO channel, with a focus on recruiting for all sales roles, enhancing the division’s process and products, and promoting the Bank’s brand on the national TPO landscape.

Mr. Constantine has been involved in TPO sales for almost 30 years, starting his career at GreenPoint Mortgage, then moving to GMAC/Ally Bank, New Penn Financial, and Ditech LLC.

Wells has held sales positions ranging from Account Executive to Regional Manager, to Divisional Manager. Most recently, he has served as National Sales manager overseeing teams whose purpose is to facilitate the growth of the residential originations platform.
