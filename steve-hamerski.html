Steve Hamerski

Assistant Vice President and TPO Central Region Sales Manager

Steve is responsible for supporting the account executive team. He works within the central region of the United States building production and promoting the brand to broker, non-delegated and delegated partnerships.

Before joining First Savings Bank, Mr. Hamerski spent over 15 years at Freedom Mortgage Corporation serving in Regional Vice President and Wholesale Account Executive roles. Prior to that, Steve worked at other major national lenders such as Washington Mutual and North American Mortgage Company.

Steve entered the TPO/Wholesale space in 1992 and has been in it ever since. He served on the Board of Directors for the Minnesota Mortgage Association for 8 years and has been an advocate for both financial institutions and independent mortgage brokers/bankers who use wholesale outlets.
