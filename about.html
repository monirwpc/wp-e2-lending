About Us

E2 Lending is a nationwide correspondent lender that specializes in building lasting relationships with brokers, lenders, and other emerging banking partners. We recognize that our success is built on the success of our partners and we strive to consistently provide a superior third-party lending experience. We offer a hands-on approach, placing people and relationships first and drive superior results for our clients and partners.

One Mission that Matters

As a symbol of trust in the Wholesale/TPO/Correspondent lending world, our mission is to create a higher quality of life for our partners, our employees, and our communities. In fact, the word TRUST is defined in the core values and guiding principles of our company.

A Note from Brian Simon, CMB, SVP and

E2 Lending and our management team would never think to tell you who you can or cannot do business with as it relates to your investor partners. We realize that competition, and the ability to have optionality in whom you choose to partner with, is the very essence of what makes you an Independent Mortgage Expert. Attempting to force a “choice,” is harmful to you, your borrowers, and our industry.

The ability for you to choose the lenders that best suit you and your borrowers is what can separate you from the rest of the market and is likely the reason being an Independent Mortgage Expert is the path you have chosen to follow. As a company, we applaud you and will always support that individual decision regardless of what lenders you choose to partner with.

Brian Simon
