HELOC

Home Equity Line of Credit

Expand your borrower’s options with our HELOC product suite. E2 Lending offers our partners a HELOC loan program in combination with a new 1st mortgage. Our in house HELOC product allows you to stand out from the crowd by giving your borrowers more purchasing power and additional refinance options.

Key Advantages

  • One underwriter, one approval, one set of conditions for both products
  • Loan amounts up to $250,000
  • FICO scores as low as 660
  • DTI up to 45 (FICO dependent)
  • Product allows for LTV up to 100% (please note we do follow 1st mortgage guidelines)
